Breakfast Recipes

Avocado Toast

Ingredients: Bread, 1 Avocado, salt, pepper, lemon juice, red pepper flakes and balsamic glaze. Supplies: a bowl, fork, knife and toaster. Instructions: Put 1/2 to 1 avocado in a bowl and start to smash it up using a fork. As it becomes softer, add in salt and pepper and squeeze in half a lemon. Mix all of the ingredients together until it is soft enough to spread onto toast. Next, toast a piece of bread and spread the mashed up avocado on top. Lastly, drizzle balsamic glaze over the avocado and eat!

Tropical Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients: 1/4 cup frozen pineapple, 1/2 frozen banana, 1/4 cup strawberries, 1/4 cup mango, ice, 1/2 cup almond milk, granola, honey and fresh fruit. Supplies: a blender, spoon and bowl. Instructions: Place the frozen fruit, almond milk and a few pieces of ice in to a blender and blend until the cosistency is smooth, yet thick. Once the smoothie has reached your desired consistency, pour it into a bowl. Next, smooth down and the top using a spoon and drizzle a little honey and put granola and fresh fruit on top, or any desired toppings. After you put on your toppings on, the smoothie bowl is complete and ready to eat!
