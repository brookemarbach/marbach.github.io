Roasted Cauliflower Tacos

Ingredients: 1 small cauliflower, 1/4 of a red cabbage, 1 tsp ground cumin, 1 tsp coriander, 1/4 cup olive oil, 1 tsp kosher salt, tortillas, red onion, avocado and cilantro. Supplies: Baking sheet, pan or toaster. Instructions: In the oven, roast cauliflower florets and cabbage together on a baking sheet, with the spices, olive oil amd salt sprinkled on top. While they are roasting, warm up your desired amount of tortillas on a pan or in a toaster. Once the veggies are done, place them in to tortillas and put red onion, avocado and cilantro on top!

Roasted Veggie Grain Bowl

Ingredients: 1 cup raw quinoa, 1 3/4 cups water, pesto, 1/2 cauliflower florets, 1/2 cup broccoli, 1/2 cup brussel sprouts, olive oil and 1/2 cup of drained & rinsed chickpeas. Supplies: baking sheet, parchment paper, medium pot and bowl. Instructions: First preheat your oven to 425Â°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Next, add the rinsed quinoa and water to a medium pot. Bring to a boil, then lower the heat and simmer, uncovered, until the quinoa appears to be ready. The next step is to roast the veggies. Put the cauliflower, broccoli and brussel sprouts on a baking sheet and drizzle olive oil on top. Place the veggies in the oven to roast for about 20 minutes. When the veggies are done, you can start to assemble your bowl. Place the quinoa, roasted vegetables and chickpeas into a bowl and your bowl is ready to eat.
