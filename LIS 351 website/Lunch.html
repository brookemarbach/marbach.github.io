Chicken Quesadilla

Ingredients: Olive oil, chicken breast, shredded cheese (mozzarella or mexican blend) and a tortilla. Supplies: A pan, knife anc cutting board. Instructions: Warm 1/2 teaspoon of oil over medium-high hear in a pan. When the oil is hot, cut up a chicken breast and add the cut up pieces of chicken in to the pan. When the chicken is fully cooked, remove it from the pan. Next, ad 1/2 teaspoon of oil on to a pan and put a tortilla in the pan. Sprinkle your desired shredded cheese and the chicken on top of half of the tortilla. Then fold the tortilla over and cook both sides until they are crispy. Finally, remove the final product from the pan and your chicken quesadilla is ready to eat!

Greek Salad

Ingredients: Sliced cucumbers, tomatoes, green bell pepper, red onion, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, olive oil, red wine vinegar, dijon mustard, garlic and dried oregano. Supplies: small bowl, whisk and large bowl. Instructions: First, start by making the dressing. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, garlic, dried oregano and dijon mustard. Next, put the sliced cucumbers, tomatoes, green bell pepper, red onion, Kalamata olives and feta cheese in a large bowl and drizzle the dressing across the top. Then mix together the ingredients in the bowl and your Greek salad is ready.
